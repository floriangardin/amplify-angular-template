Data Governance Organization

Below is the Data Governance Organization for your company. You'll interact with several of these roles throughout the simulations.

Key roles

  • Amira Collins — Chief Data Officer (CDO)

    Heads the Data Governance Office. Defines the overall data strategy and policies, and ensures alignment across business domains.

  • Sarah Jang — Data Owner (Customer Domain)

    Accountable for the quality, definitions, and correct usage of data in her domain. Approves rules and priorities proposed by Data Stewards.

  • Leo Nguyen — IT Data Engineer

    Implements data pipelines, applies technical validations, and manages database environments. Works closely with Data Stewards and Data Owners.

  • Leah Park — Marketing Manager

    Represents business data consumers. Provides feedback on data usability and reports issues related to campaigns and customer segmentation.

  • Raj Patel — Data Quality Analyst

    Monitors data quality indicators, produces dashboards, and suggests corrective actions based on metrics.
